Pasta recipe

Description

This is a pasta with shrimps recipe

It's simple and quick to make

Ingredients

  • Raw pasta
  • Frozen shrimps
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Parsley
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Boil water, add salt to it and once boiled add raw pasta and cook for 20 minutes on low/medium heat
  2. While water for pasta is boiling, add olive oil to the pan and add frozen shrimps
  3. Season shrimps with salt, pepper and garlic powder and cook for about half an hour on medium heat
  4. Once pasta is cooked remove water and serve into a dish
  5. Add cooked shrimps on top of pasta and add parsley on top
