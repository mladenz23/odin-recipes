Steak recipe

Description

This is a steak recipe

You want to know how to cook a perfect steak? Here's how!

Ingredients

  • Sirloin steak
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Thyme

Steps

  1. Season steak with salt and pepper and let it marinate over night
  2. Put olive oil in a pan and add seasoned steak on hot pan
  3. Add garlic and thyme for taste
  4. Add butter and flip steak to cook evenly on both sides
  5. Cook for about 8 minutes
  6. After the steak is cooked, wrap it in tin foil and leave it for 10 minutes
