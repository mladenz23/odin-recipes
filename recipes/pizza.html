Pizza recipe

Description

Wanna make the best italian pizza?

This is how!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Mushrooms
  • Cheery tomatoes
  • Basil leaves
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Roll out the dough
  2. Spread tomato sauce over dough
  3. Add desired amount of cheese on top of the sauce
  4. Add mushrooms, tomatoes and olive oil
  5. Cook for 10 minutes on 240Â°C until crisp
  6. Serve with a bit more olive oil and basil leaves
